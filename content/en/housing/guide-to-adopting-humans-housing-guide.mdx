### 🏠 Introduction to Adopting Humans Housing

In the game "Guide to Adopting Humans," building a home for your adoptees is a crucial aspect of creating a welcoming environment. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, this guide will help you build stylish and functional homes that reflect your personal style.

### 🏘️ Choosing the Perfect House

The first step in building a home is to choose the right house. The Queen's Lander house is a popular choice due to its preppy aesthetics and spacious design. For beginners, consider starting with a smaller house like the Tiny Home or the Family House to get a feel for the building process.

### 🎨 Choosing Preppy Colors

Preppy homes are known for their light and pastel colors. When choosing colors for your home, opt for light pink, light yellow, light blue, and light purple. These colors will give your home a fresh and inviting look.

### 🛋️ Keeping Furniture

Don't sell all the furniture in your house. Instead, keep the furniture that you will need and put the rest in a separate room. This will help you save bugs and make your home look more organized.

### 🛠️ Customizing Walls

Adopt Me! provides a variety of wall options, but they can be quite expensive. To save bugs, search for "Deluxe rug" and use it to decorate your walls. It's a cheaper and cuter alternative that can give your home a unique look.

### 🎨 Customizing Floors

For floors, search for "double floor panel" and choose a color that matches your home's theme. Use the advanced tools feature to clone the floor and ensure that it doesn't glitch.

### 🌟 Adding Preppy Touches

To add a preppy touch to your home, consider adding mirrors, lockers, and clutter. Clutter can include surfboards, stars, hearts, wheels, windmills, walking horses, lockers, palm trees, chopping boards, lights, chairs, pet displays, and rugs.

### 🛠️ Preppy Building Hacks

To learn more preppy building hacks, check out the "tofy Twins" channel on YouTube. They have a playlist dedicated to preppy building hacks that can help you create stunning homes.

### 🚪 Adding a Grinding Area

A grinding area is a great way to fill up space and make your home more functional. You can add food, water beds, a litter tray, a bath, and a piano to create a cozy grinding area.

### 🌈 Adding a Preppy Pathway

To add a preppy pathway, use the "General Pathway" and decorate it with stars, wheels, chopping boards, neon arrow lights, and plates. This will create a charming entrance to your home.

### 🏘️ Conclusion

Building a preppy home in "Guide to Adopting Humans" can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following these tips and tricks, you can create a stylish and functional home that your adoptees will love. Happy building!
