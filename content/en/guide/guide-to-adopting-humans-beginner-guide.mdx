### 📚 Introduction to Adopting Humans

Welcome to the ultimate beginner's guide to Adopt Me! If you're new to the game and want to get started on the right foot, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know to adopt humans, manage your family, and build a thriving community in Adopt Me.

### 🎯 Setting Up Your Character

The first step in Adopt Me is to set up your character. Once you join the game, you'll be given a basic character with a default appearance. It's important to dress up your character to avoid scams and make a good impression. You can change your appearance by visiting the clothing store and selecting items from your inventory.

**Tip**: Always dress up your character before joining the game to avoid being targeted by scammers.

### 🎯 Daily Tasks and Rewards

One of the key aspects of Adopt Me is completing daily tasks for rewards. These tasks can range from simple actions like logging in every day to more complex tasks like adopting a new human or completing a special event. Each task you complete will earn you in-game currency, which you can use to buy houses, eggs, and other items.

**Important**: Always check the task board for daily rewards and opportunities. Completing tasks can significantly speed up your progress in the game.

### 🎯 Managing Your Human Family

As you adopt humans, it's important to manage your family effectively. Each human has their own needs and wants, and it's your job to make them happy. You can do this by completing their tasks, buying them gifts, and providing them with a loving home.

**Tip**: Prioritize tasks that make the most money and help you progress in the game. This will help you build a strong and prosperous family.

### 🎯 Adopting Humans

Adopting humans is a key part of the Adopt Me experience. You can adopt humans from the nursery or by trading with other players. When adopting a human, it's important to consider their personality and needs. Some humans may be more demanding than others, so it's important to be prepared for the challenge.

**Important**: Always check the human's profile before adopting them to ensure they are a good fit for your family.

### 🎯 Trading and Trusting

Trading and trusting are important aspects of Adopt Me. You can trade pets, items, and even humans with other players. However, it's important to be cautious when trading, as there are scammers out there who may try to take advantage of you.

**Tip**: Never trust trade and always perform fair trades. Check the value of the items or humans you're trading to ensure you're getting a good deal.

### 🎯 Jobs and Drops

Adopt Me offers a variety of jobs that you can complete to earn money and experience. These jobs include being a waiter, a stylist, a doctor, and more. Each job has its own set of tasks and rewards, so it's important to choose the job that best suits your playstyle.

**Important**: Some jobs allow you to stay AFK (away from keyboard) and still earn money, which can be a great way to make money while you're busy with other tasks.

### 🎯 Eggs and Legendaries

Adopt Me offers a variety of eggs that you can hatch to get new pets. Some eggs have a higher chance of hatching legendary pets, which are highly sought-after by players. It's important to understand the different types of eggs and their chances of hatching legendary pets.

**Tip**: Consider the rarity and value of the pets you're hatching. Some pets may be worth more than others, so it's important to choose wisely.

### 🎯 Conclusion

Adopt Me is a fun and engaging game that offers a unique experience for players of all ages. By following this beginner's guide, you'll be well on your way to adopting humans, managing your family, and building a thriving community in Adopt Me. Remember to prioritize tasks, manage your resources effectively, and always be cautious when trading and adopting humans.
