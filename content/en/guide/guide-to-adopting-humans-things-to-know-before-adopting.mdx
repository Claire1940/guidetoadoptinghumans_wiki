## 🎮 Guide to Adopting Humans: Everything You Need to Know Before Adopting

Are you considering adopting a human? Whether it's your first time or you're looking to expand your family, there are many things to consider before taking the plunge. In this comprehensive guide, we'll cover everything you need to know to ensure a successful adoption process and a happy life for both you and your new human companion.

### 🌟 Quick Guide
- **Primary keyword**: Guide to Adopting Humans
- **Key mechanic**: Understanding the responsibilities and considerations of adoption
- **Best strategy**: Research, patience, and preparation
- **Critical tip**: Always consider the well-being of the human before making the decision
- **Top choice**: Adopting from a shelter or rescue organization

### 🏡 Preparing Your Home

#### 🎉 Securing Your Space
Before bringing your new human home, it's essential to secure your living space. Here's what you need to do:

- **Kitten-proof your home**: Secure breakable objects, block off fireplaces, and consider putting baby proof locks on doors.
- **Provide a safe haven**: Create a quiet and comfortable space where your human can retreat when they need a moment to themselves.

### 🌙 Understanding Your Human's Needs

#### 🎭 Kitten's Playtime
Kittens are high-energy and require plenty of playtime. Here are some tips:

- **Engage with toys**: Invest in fun toys to keep your human entertained.
- **Interactive play**: Spend time playing with your human to strengthen your bond and provide exercise.

### 🌟 Nighttime Considerations

#### 🌜 Crepuscular Behavior
Contrary to popular belief, cats are not nocturnal. They are most active at dusk and dawn. Here's what you should know:

- **Adjust your routine**: Prepare for your human to be up and active during these times.
- **Find a solution**: Consider locking your human out of your bedroom at night or engaging in play sessions to tire them out.

### 🍖 Feeding and Nutrition

#### 🥢 Kitten's Diet
Feeding your human a balanced diet is crucial for their health and well-being. Here are some tips:

- **Soft food is best**: Provide soft food with moisture for a more natural diet.
- **Consider their preferences**: Some humans may be more food motivated than others.

### 🌱 Safety and Health

#### 🐾 Keeping Your Human Safe
Safety is a top priority when adopting a human. Here are some tips:

- **No outside**: Keep your human indoors to protect them from dangers like cars, predators, and toxic substances.
- **Consider catios**: If you want your human to have some outdoor time, consider installing a catio or leash training them.

### 🏡 Post-Adoption Care

#### 🛌 Bonding with Your Human
Building a strong bond with your human is essential for a successful adoption. Here are some tips:

- **Quality time**: Spend time together every day to strengthen your bond.
- **Understanding their needs**: Be patient and understand that your human will have different needs than a traditional cat.

### 🌟 FAQs

#### What happens if I regret my human choice?
Once you adopt a human, the decision is permanent. However, you can create a new human if you want to explore a different type of companion.

#### Can I play as a human but use a different type of food?
Yes, you can, but it may not be as effective as choosing a human that complements that playstyle.

#### Which type of human is best for solo play?
Mage is ideal for solo play due to their powerful spells and range attacks. However, Rogue also works well if you want more mobility and a hybrid playstyle.
