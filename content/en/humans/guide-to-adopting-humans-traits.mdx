## 🏰 Introduction to the Beast Master Class

The Beast Master class in Once Human 2.0 is a unique and powerful class that allows you to command up to three pets to gather resources for you and dominate in battle. This class comes with a variety of traits and abilities that make it an exciting choice for players looking to explore the wild side of Once Human 2.0.

### 🐾 Choosing the Right Pets

When starting out as a Beast Master, you have the option to choose between three different pets: Pongo, Misha, and Shirou. Each pet has its own strengths and weaknesses, so it's important to choose the one that best suits your playstyle.

- **Pongo**: A versatile pet for foraging and farming, perfect for players who enjoy gathering resources.
- **Misha**: A tanky pet that excels in endgame content, ideal for players who want a reliable frontline tank.
- **Shirou**: A DPS-focused pet that can quickly take out enemies, perfect for players who prioritize damage output.

## 🐯 Capturing the Coolest Animals

Once you have your pet, it's time to start capturing the coolest animals in the game. The key to success is to focus on high-grade animals, as they offer the best traits and power-ups.

### 🎯 Animal Capture and Taming

To capture animals, you'll need to craft different darts, such as tranquilizer arrows and tranquilizer darts. Once you've captured an animal, you can tame it by keeping it well-fed and increasing its tameness level.

### 🍖 Animal Food and Snacks

To keep your animals well-fed, you'll need to craft different types of food, such as meat, grain, and herbal food. Additionally, you can craft snacks to boost your animals' performance and increase their tameness level.

## 🛠️ Pet Power-Ups

Once you've captured and tamed your pets, you can enhance their abilities by equipping them with power-ups. These power-ups include the metal claw, the beast carropus, the metal dental set, and the animal backpack.

### 🐾 Binding Pets to Whistles

You can also bind your pets to specific whistles, allowing you to easily summon and control them in battle. This feature is particularly useful when dealing with multiple pets at once.

## 🌟 Farming Routes for the Best Pets

To get the most out of your Beast Master class, it's important to know the best farming routes for the best pets. Here are some of the top spots to find high-grade animals:

- **Bears**: The Moon Lake TP tower in the northwestern part of Chalk Peak.
- **Wolves**: The Blackhearts Bay TP tower in the northern part of the desert.
- **Leo parts**: Wishland, specifically between Wishland West and Wishland East.

## 🌟 Mutating Genetics

One of the most interesting aspects of the Beast Master class is the ability to mutate the genetics of animals. By capturing and slaughtering animals with specific traits, you can craft mutagens that enhance your pets' abilities.

### 🌟 Crafting Mutagens

Some of the best mutagens to focus on include:

- **Fire Trap Mutagen**: Adds a blazing damage effect to your creatures.
- **Frostborn Mutagen**: Adds frost damage to your creatures.
- **Pollution Mutagen**: Deals additional status damage to your creatures.
